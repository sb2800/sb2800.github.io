Welcome to Bright Owls! We are a literacy initiative offering resources, tips and fun activities to promote reading among children. Our goal is to help and support literacy in young students through fun activities, accessible reading materials and interactive literacy lessons. Bright Owls also offers resources to parents and educators so that they too may help their students and children become stronger readers.

Little Writers

A picture can be worth a thousand words! Have your child snap a few photos of things he or she likes around the neighborhood and talk about them. Encourage the child to ask questions about the pictures: can he or she think of any characters that could go in the photo? What is in the setting? What type of story could be inspired by the picture? A little visual aid could be a useful jumpstart to a great story!
